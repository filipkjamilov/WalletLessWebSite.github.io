WalletLess App

WalletLess app is a the perfect place to store all your loyalty cards at one place! Easy to use and convenient application that allows you to add all your loyalty cards in this virtual wallet. All cards available even if you are offline. The cards are sorted by the user location for even more easy to use!

Why WalletLess?

  • The card you need always on top!

How it started?

The first idea came with the problem that our wallets were becoming bigger and bigger each day. Every supermarket, food shop, cinema, pharmacy have they're own loyalty cards. With this loyalty cards you have your own rewards such as collecting points, membership, discounts and more. We all love rewards, but it get's frustrating to carry so many loyalty cards in your pockets.
Let's admit! It's frustrating!
